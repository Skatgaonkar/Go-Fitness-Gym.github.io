5 Tips for Making Healthy Happen Outside the Gym

The success of any good workout plan has more to do with what you do outside of training then when you’re actually training. If you think about it, a 1 hour workout is somewhere between 4-5% of your total day. This means that about 95% of your day is spent outside of the gym and can have a bigger impact on your fitness journey than any single training session you will ever complete.
